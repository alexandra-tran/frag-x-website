weapons

  • rail gun (rg)

    • a weapon which goes in a straight line instantly and hits the first player that that line intersects with, if it hits a wall it stops there

    • fire rate: 1 shot per second
  • rocket launcher (rl)

    • a weapon which goes in a straight line at a constant speed, if it hits a wall then an explosion is created

    • fire rate: 1 shot per second
